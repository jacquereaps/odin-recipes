Homemade Lemonade

Description

The best, simply. Quench your thirst with this delicious sipper of a drink!

Ingredients

  • Hot Water
  • Cold Water
  • Ice
  • Sugar
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Add desired sugar to a glass and dissolve with hot water
  2. Squeeze at least one whole lemon into the glass
  3. Fill with cold water
  4. Top with ice. Enjoy!
