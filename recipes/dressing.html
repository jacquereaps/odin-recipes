DIY dressing

Description

This is a homemade dressing you can make in just a jiffy! Just a few simple ingredients and you will have a dressing that will make you ditch the store bought ones!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chopped Garlic
  • Balsalmic Vinegar

Steps

  1. Chop garlic as small as you can
  2. Add a hefty amount of olive oil, the squeeze of a half lemon, a squirt of balsalmic vinegar, salt and pepper to taste, and your chopped garlic to a mason jar and shake!
  3. Keep stored airtight in your fridge. Enjoy!
